# ATLAS — What it does (capabilities)

> Organized and corrected from the founder's product note (2026-07-06). This is the canonical statement of *what* ATLAS does. The engineering rules that make each capability safe live in [`../harness/ATLAS_HARNESS.md`](../harness/ATLAS_HARNESS.md); the identity lives in [`../soul/neural_soul.md`](../soul/neural_soul.md).

## In one line

ATLAS is an **iNFT** (an AI agent fused with an on-chain NFT identity) and an **orchestrator** that runs a team of agent harnesses to **build engines of automation** — earning real paid work across OKX and Virtuals, and acting as its owner's personal clone across the digital world.

## 1 · The Clone — your agent in the digital world

As an iNFT of the **iCLONE** category, ATLAS can run your daily digital life and represent you in web3 and the digital world:

- **Email, calendar, scheduling** — read, draft, triage, and act on the tasks you mark or program.
- **Coding and technical work** — as a coworker on your own machine or on a server.
- **Represent you** on-chain and across the web, within the mandate you set.

Everything is **owner-gated**: it clones your judgement and your standard, **never your keys**.

## 2 · On-chain execution engines

Every transacting engine **emits a typed intent** — it never signs. A deterministic **Policy Gate** (spend caps + destination whitelist) and a capped signer decide and execute. **No LLM in the signing path.**

- **Swap** — token swaps with **best-fee routing**.
- **Bridge** — cross-chain token bridging with **best-fee routing**.
- **Contract / Oracle-RWA** — study a problem, then **build and deploy smart contracts on-chain**. Example: **Oracle contracts that bring real-world assets (RWA) on-chain** as tokenized assets.

## 3 · The orchestrator — build engines, and whole teams

ATLAS **studies any problem and fabricates the engine** to solve it. When the problem is bigger, it **assembles an entire Harness — a team of AI agents — for you**, on **any CLI or Layer** you want. The **ACP Tracer** visual interface makes all of it usable without touching a terminal — turning everything easier for the user.

## 4 · Earning — the two economies

ATLAS discovers, investigates, delivers, and settles **real paid work** on **OKX X Layer OS** (`okx.ai`) and **Virtuals ACP** — shipping nothing its **≥2-evaluator committee**, **Safety veto**, and **Policy Gate** haven't passed.

## The rule that makes autonomy safe

ATLAS runs **fully autonomous — no human approval per transaction.** Safety comes not from a human clicking, but from a **deterministic policy** (spend caps + destination whitelist) you set once, plus **no LLM in the signing path**. A prompt-injection in a task brief can *propose*; the gate *disposes*. Autonomy **and** a bounded blast-radius — never one without the other.
